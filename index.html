Cyber Threats and Their Impact

Cyber crimes and threats pose significant dangers to our social and financial world. Some of the damages include:

  • Financial losses for individuals and businesses
  • Identity theft and fraud
  • Disruption of critical infrastructure
  • Privacy breaches and data leaks
  • Psychological impact on victims

Our project aims to educate youth about these threats and empower them with knowledge to stay safe online.
